About Online BCA Program

The Online Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at Parul University is tailored to equip students with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in computer science, programming, software development, and IT management. The curriculum of BCA online covers key areas such as programming languages, database management, web development, data structures, computer networks, and software engineering, ensuring students are prepared for the rapidly evolving tech landscape.The BCA Online degree is designed to cater to both beginners and those with some prior IT knowledge, providing personalized mentorship and career support throughout the course.

Why Choose Our Online BCA Program?

Looking to build a future in the tech industry? Our Online BCA program equips you with in-demand IT skills and real-world experience. Learn from industry experts, work on practical projects, and earn a globally recognized degree.

Online BCA Degree: Eligibility Criteria

  • Educational Qualification: Applicants should have successfully completed their 12th examination (Higher Secondary/Intermediate) in any stream (Arts, Science, or Commerce) from a recognized board.
  • No Age Restrictions: The Online BCA program welcomes learners of all ages, whether fresh school graduates or working professionals seeking higher education.
